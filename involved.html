Campus Ambassador

The Roles and Responsibilities:
● Representing inState amongst societies at your respective universities.
● Liaising with inState members, university students and university societies to advertise inState opportunities and events.
● Organising mentees and mentors at your respective university.
● Working closely with other Campus Ambassadors across the UK.
● Acting in a communicative role on behalf of University Liaison Coordinators to reach members/students/societies at your university. This will include regular meetings with a member of the Liaison Coordinator team.
● Familiarisation and understanding of our mission and values to act in an educative role to students/societies at your university

Pathway Coordinator

● Designing the Pathway’s mentorship scheme, with the rest of the team
○ Selecting mentors and mentees to take part in this scheme
○ Choosing content/structure of the programme

● Liaising with external professionals to extend inState’s network

Internal Strategy Advisory

The strategy team at inState is made up of a group of students from different corporate backgrounds who advise on the direction of the organisation. The strategy team advise on individual pathway divisions, as we have key strategy pathway links, as well as the broader strategic considerations of the organisation, including operational marketing, external affairs and organisational finances.

Roles and Responsibilities:
● Advising on organisational strategy/vision broadly
● Leading on strategy document creation for internal and external reference
● Creating a timeline of key deliverables for operational roles to fulfil
● Liaising with external strategy advisory board and recruitment
● Managing the inState contacts documents, key for recruitment and networking opportunities for our members
● Providing strategic oversight to individual pathways through the strategy pathway links role
● Help informing mentorship and professional development content
● Reaching out to external professionals
● Advising on social media and exclusive web app content for the individual pathways
● Advising Design and Development on the construction of the inState Web App and other related questions
● Leading on internal legal and finance functions
● Organisational constitution
● Membership contracts
● Advising on data protection and training the team on GDPR regulations
● Code of conducts document
● Incorporating as a charity
● Corporate sponsorships and funding formulas
